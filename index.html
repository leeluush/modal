Hey, I am a modal!

This is an example of a modal window implemented using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can use modals to display important messages, forms, or any other content that requires user interaction without navigating away from the current page.

Modals are a great way to keep users engaged and ensure that critical information is noticed. They are often used for login forms, alerts, or confirmations.
